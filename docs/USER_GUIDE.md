## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. **Visit the Registration Page**
   - Go to [designermarketplace.com/register](https://designermarketplace.com/register)
   - Or click "Sign Up" in the navigation bar

2. **Enter Your Information**
   - Email address
   - Password (minimum 8 characters)
   - First and last name
   - Account type (Buyer or Seller)

3. **Verify Your Email**
   - Check your inbox for a verification email
   - Click the verification link
   - Your account is now active!

4. **Complete Profile Setup**
   - Add a profile photo
   - Write a brief bio
   - Set your preferences

> üí° **Tip**: Use a professional email address and real name for better trust with other users.

### Profile Setup

Your profile is your first impression. Make it count!

**Essential Profile Elements:**

| Element | Buyers | Sellers |
|---------|--------|---------|
| Profile Photo | Recommended | Required |
| Display Name | Optional | Required |
| Bio | Optional | Required |
| Skills | N/A | Required |
| Portfolio | N/A | Highly Recommended |

**How to Edit Your Profile:**

1. Click your avatar in the top right
2. Select "Profile Settings"
3. Update your information
4. Click "Save Changes"

### Ordering a Service

#### Step 1: Choose a Gig

1. Browse or search for the service you need
2. Click on a gig to view details
3. Review the designer's:
   - Portfolio samples
   - Package options
   - Reviews from other buyers
   - Response time

#### Step 2: Select a Package

Most gigs offer three packages:

| Package | Best For |
|---------|----------|
| **Basic** | Simple projects, tight budgets |
| **Standard** | Most common choice, balanced value |
| **Premium** | Complex projects, priority support |

Compare what's included in each package before deciding.

#### Step 3: Place Your Order

1. Click "Continue" on your chosen package
2. Fill out the requirements form:
   - Describe your project in detail
   - Upload reference files if available
   - Answer the designer's questions
3. Review your order summary
4. Complete payment

#### Step 4: Track Your Order

After ordering, you can:
- View order status in "My Orders"
- Communicate with the designer via messages
- Track progress and updates
- Receive notifications for milestones

## For Sellers (Designers)

### Becoming a Seller

#### Requirements

- Verified email address
- Complete profile with photo
- At least one skill selected
- Portfolio samples (recommended)

#### Steps to Get Started

1. **Upgrade Your Account**
   - Go to Profile Settings
   - Click "Become a Seller"
   - Complete seller onboarding

2. **Set Up Your Seller Profile**
   - Professional bio (250+ words)
   - Skills and expertise
   - Portfolio pieces
   - Pricing expectations

3. **Create Your First Gig**
   - Follow our gig creation guide below
   - Start with your best service
   - Price competitively for initial orders

### Creating Gigs

#### Gig Structure

Every gig needs:

1. **Title** (10-100 characters)
   - Clear and specific
   - Include main service
   - Example: "I will design a modern minimalist logo for your brand"

2. **Category & Subcategory**
   - Choose the most relevant
   - Helps buyers find you

3. **Description** (100-5000 characters)
   - What's included
   - Your process
   - What makes you unique
   - Delivery format

4. **Packages** (1-3 options)
   - Basic: Entry-level offering
   - Standard: Best value
   - Premium: Complete solution

5. **Pricing**
   - Research competition
   - Start competitive
   - Increase as you get reviews

6. **Gallery**
   - High-quality images
   - Before/after examples
   - Video (optional but powerful)

7. **Tags**
   - Relevant keywords
   - Max 5 tags
   - Think like a buyer

#### Creating Packages

| Element | Basic | Standard | Premium |
|---------|-------|----------|---------|
| Price | $$ | $$$ | $$$$ |
| Concepts | 1 | 2-3 | 5+ |
| Revisions | 1-2 | 3-5 | Unlimited |
| Delivery | 5-7 days | 3-5 days | 1-3 days |
| Files | PNG | PNG + Vector | All formats |
| Extras | - | Source files | + Brand guide |

#### Gig SEO Tips

1. **Title Optimization**
   - Lead with "I will"
   - Include main keyword
   - Be specific about outcome

2. **Description Keywords**
   - Natural keyword usage
   - Include variations
   - Focus on benefits

3. **Tags Strategy**
   - Primary service tag
   - Style tags
   - Industry tags

## FAQ

### General

**Q: Is there a mobile app?**
A: Yes! Download from the App Store or Google Play.

**Q: What currencies are supported?**
A: USD, EUR, GBP, and 20+ other currencies.

**Q: How do I change my username?**
A: Go to Profile Settings > Edit Profile > Display Name.

### For Buyers

**Q: Can I get a refund?**
A: Refunds are available for incomplete orders or if the work doesn't match the description. Contact support for assistance.

**Q: How do revisions work?**
A: Each package includes a set number of revisions. Request changes before accepting delivery.

**Q: What if the designer doesn't respond?**
A: You can cancel the order if there's no response within 48 hours.

### For Sellers

**Q: When can I withdraw my earnings?**
A: After the 14-day clearance period following order completion.

**Q: How can I increase my visibility?**
A: Maintain high ratings, respond quickly, and optimize your gig descriptions.

**Q: Can I refuse an order?**
A: You can cancel with mutual agreement, but frequent cancellations affect your ranking.
